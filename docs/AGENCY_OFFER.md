## What you're actually selling

Each customer gets a GHL **sub-account under your agency**, pre-loaded with the
automations below, branded MaritimeGeo. You set it up once (it's a snapshot —
build it one time, clone it for every customer). They pay monthly.

The five automations local businesses actually pay for — each one is a concrete,
demo-able result:

1. **Missed-Call Text-Back** — someone calls, you're on a ladder, they instantly
   get "Sorry we missed you — what do you need?" texted back. Recovers jobs that
   would've gone to the next Google result. *This is the #1 seller. Lead with it.*
2. **Review Machine** — job marked done → automatic Google-review request text,
   with a polite chase. More reviews → more calls. Visible result in weeks.
3. **Speed-to-Lead** — every web/Facebook/GBP enquiry gets an instant text +
   email reply and lands in one pipeline, so no lead sits unanswered overnight.
4. **Appointment Reminders / No-Show Killer** — booking confirmations and
   day-before reminders, automatic.
5. **Database Reactivation** — a friendly "it's been a while, want a quote on
   X?" campaign to their old customer list. Often pays for the whole service
   with one reactivated job. (Their OWN past customers — consent is theirs, so
   it's CASL-clean.)

**AI, where it's real:** GHL's conversation AI can answer common questions and
book appointments by text ("AI receptionist"). Sell it as exactly that — an
after-hours receptionist — not as "AI everything."

**ProfitProof is your closer:** every other agency says "trust us, it works."
You say "here's the dashboard that ties every lead to the invoice it became."
Proof is your differentiator. (Skip "security" and "art" — not this business.)

## Email sending — the rules that keep you safe & effective

- **You send, or you approve every send.** Claude drafts personalized emails and
  places them in your Gmail as DRAFTS; you tap Send on each. Human-approved,
  one-to-one, to people/businesses you have a real connection to = fine.
- **No cold blasts to scraped lists.** CASL (Canada's anti-spam law) has real
  fines, and blasts don't convert anyway. Ten personal emails beat a thousand
  robot ones.
- Every email: your real name, real business, and "just reply 'not interested'
  and I won't follow up" — which is both polite and CASL-friendly.
- Where automation IS the answer: GHL workflows for everyone who **replies or
  opts in** (see SALES_KIT.md), and reactivation campaigns to a business's
  **own** customer list inside their sub-account.
