# Selling your GHL automations as digital products (Gumroad & friends)

The model: you build a workflow bundle ONCE in your GoHighLevel agency account,
export it as a **snapshot share link**, and sell that link (plus an install
guide) as an instant-delivery digital product. The marketplace handles payment,
delivery, and receipts — nothing to ship, nothing to schedule. This is the most
couch-compatible income in this whole plan.

**Honest expectations first:** listing a product is not the same as selling it.
Snapshot sellers who make money are visible in GHL communities (Facebook groups,
Reddit r/gohighlevel, the official community). Plan to post value there weekly.
Realistic early numbers: a handful of $37–$197 sales/month at first, growing
with reviews and posts — real money, not a lottery ticket.

### INSTALL GUIDE (ship this with every product)

**What you bought:** a GoHighLevel snapshot — a pre-built bundle of workflows,
pipelines, and messages that installs into your GHL account in a few clicks.

**You need:** a GoHighLevel account (any paid plan) with an agency view, or ask
whoever manages your GHL to do these steps. Install takes ~10 minutes.

**Steps:**
1. Log into GHL **Agency View**.
2. Open the snapshot link from your purchase. GHL will ask which account to
   import into — choose your agency.
3. Go to **Agency → Account Snapshots** — you'll see the imported snapshot.
4. Create a new sub-account from it (or push it into an existing sub-account):
   **Sub-Accounts → the account → Actions → Load Snapshot**.
5. Connect your phone number: **Settings → Phone Numbers** (buy/port a number
   through LC Phone or Twilio if you haven't).
6. Connect email sending: **Settings → Email Services**.
7. Open **Automation → Workflows** — every workflow from the bundle is there,
   set to Draft. Read each one, personalize the message text with your business
   name, then flip each to **Publish**.
8. **Test it on yourself:** call your GHL number from your cell and don't answer
   — you should get the text-back within seconds. Submit your own form — you
   should get the instant reply.

**If something doesn't fire:** 90% of the time it's (a) workflow still in
Draft, (b) phone number not connected, or (c) trigger filters pointing at the
wrong pipeline. Check those three first.

**Support:** email [your email] with "SNAPSHOT" in the subject. I'm a real
person (a painter, actually) and I answer.

## Where to list (in order of effort-to-payoff)

1. **Gumroad** — start here. Free to list, instant file/link delivery,
   handles VAT/receipts. gumroad.com → Start selling → New product →
   "Digital product" → attach the PDF(s) → set price → Publish.
2. **Whop** — marketplace where automation/SaaS templates sell well.
3. **Etsy (digital download)** — surprisingly real traffic for "GoHighLevel
   snapshot" and "small business CRM template" searches. Same files.
4. **GHL Facebook groups & r/gohighlevel** — not a marketplace, but where the
   buyers are. Post value (screenshots of the text-back working), link in
   comments. One good post outsells a week of silence.
5. Later, if the products sell: the official **HighLevel Marketplace**
   (submission + review process; do it once you have reviews elsewhere).

## Your build checklist (the only parts that need YOUR hands)

1. In GHL Agency View: build the workflows in ONE clean sub-account (use GHL's
   own recipe templates: Automation → Workflows → the recipe library has
   missed-call text-back and review request ready to customize).
2. **Agency → Account Snapshots → Create Snapshot** from that sub-account.
3. **Share → Permalink** — that link is the product.
4. Snapshot the two smaller subsets the same way (duplicate the sub-account,
   delete what's not in the tier, snapshot again).
5. Make the two PDFs (guide above + link file), open Gumroad, paste the listing
   copy, attach, publish.
6. Post once in one GHL Facebook group. Repeat weekly.
